Earth is the third rock from the sun. Also the planet we all live in. It is the only planet that we know supports life. It is able to do this because it exists in the Goldilocks zone, an area a certain distance from the sun with the ability to form life.It is also the only planet where water exists in three states of matter. Life forms on this planet are usually carbon based. Water is so useful that all life needs it in order to function. You can click and drag on the image to see the planets in detail.
