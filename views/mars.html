Mars is the fourth planet from the sun. It is also the furthest planet that can be considered in the inner circle of planets in our solar system. The largest volcano in our solar system is located here called Olympus Mons. In addition, Mars has two moons called Deimos and Phobos, which are more like asteroids than actual moons.
